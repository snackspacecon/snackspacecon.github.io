HINT: Not all pages are meant to be found through normal navigation. Some are hidden from human visitors.

CHALLENGE 02: SHADOW CTF

Infiltrate our network through a multi-stage capture the flag challenge. Begin by finding hidden data in the website's shadows, then discover our hidden access point and navigate the terminal maze.

HINT: Shadows exist in the paths between rendered pixels. Look at what forms the image, not what appears on screen.

Navigate a complex web of hidden DNS records to uncover secrets buried in the internet's infrastructure. Start with a single domain and follow the digital breadcrumbs across record types, encoded messages, and steganographic IP addresses.

HINT: The journey begins at start.hunt.snackspacecon.com - all tools needed are freely available.
